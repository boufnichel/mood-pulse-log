# Privacy Policy — Mood Pulse Log
_Last updated: 2025_

Mood Pulse Log respects your privacy. This app does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your mood entries and notes are kept **locally on your device** and remain fully under your control.

## Information We Collect
Mood Pulse Log does **not** collect or process:
- Personal information  
- Contact details  
- Location data  
- Usage analytics  
- Advertising identifiers  
- Any data stored in your mood logs  

No data is shared with third parties.

## Data Storage
All information you enter (moods, notes, trends) is stored **only on your device**.  
Deleting the app removes all data permanently.

## Third-Party Services
Mood Pulse Log does not use:
- Tracking technologies  
- Cloud storage  
- External analytics  
- Advertising networks  

## Children’s Privacy
The app does not collect data from anyone, including children.
